Support Us in Cleaning Our Beaches

Your support is crucial in our mission to protect our coastal environments and marine ecosystems. By contributing to our cause, you become an essential part of the solution to beach pollution and marine debris. Here's how you can make a difference:

Donate

Every dollar counts in our fight against ocean pollution:

  • $1 removes one pound of trash from our beaches and rivers
  • Your donations fund cleanup equipment and educational materials
  • Support the development of innovative cleanup technologies

Corporate Partnerships

Businesses can play a significant role in our mission:

  • Sponsor beach cleanup events
  • Organize employee volunteer days
  • Provide in-kind donations of supplies or services
