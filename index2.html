Beach Cleaner, a dedicated group of volunteers committed to preserving our coastal environments through beach cleaning initiatives. We are a passionate community of individuals united by our love for the ocean and our determination to protect it from the growing threat of pollution.


Our mission is to remove harmful debris from our shorelines, raise awareness about ocean pollution, and inspire action in our communities. Since our founding, we have organized countless beach cleanups, engaging volunteers of all ages and backgrounds in this crucial environmental effort.


At Beach Cleaner, we believe that every piece of litter removed from our beaches makes a difference. Our volunteers scan the sand for various types of debris, from plastic bags to microplastics, preventing this waste from entering the ocean and harming marine life.
